ORBIT (DEEP SPACE PROTOCOL)
​THE VIBE Think of this as a "think-ahead" space puzzler. It’s not about fast reflexes; it’s about understanding the invisible pull of the universe. It feels professional because it doesn’t cheat on the math.

​THE PHYSICS (INTERMEDIATE+) - True Gravity: Unlike most games that just "fake" movement, this uses a real 1/r^2 gravity formula. The closer you are to a mass, the faster you get pulled.

​Vector Math: Every frame, the game calculates the combined pull of every planet on your ship. It creates these beautiful, sweeping curves that look like actual NASA trajectory maps.

​WHAT MAKES IT "ULTRA PRO": - 100 Levels: A built-in "Architect" creates a unique challenge for every level, slowly adding more "Dead Star" hazards.

​Auto-Memory: It saves exactly which sector you’re on. You can close the tab and come back weeks later, and you're still in the same spot.
​Kinetic Polish: The neon trails and screen shakes when you hit a hazard make it feel like a finished product, not just a school project.
